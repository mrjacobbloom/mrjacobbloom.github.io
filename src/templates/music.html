My name's Jacob and I've been involved in musical performances all my life. I'm a singer, pianist, and amatuer music producer. Some of my work and accomplishments are detailed below.

A Cappella / Choir

Since my freshman year of college, I've been involved in an a cappella group called In the Buff. It's honestly been one of the best decisions I've made in college. I met some of my best friends through the group and it's a highlight of my week.

In 2017, ITB competed in the regional quarterfinal of the ICCA (the competition from Pitch Perfect). We came in third with an award for best arrangement, and more importantly, we had a great time doing it.

We're currently in the process of recording an album. It'll be our first album in over seven years. Check out ITB's website and social media for info on how that's coming along, or where you can buy it.

I've had previous choir experience as well. In high school, I was briefly co-president of the jazz choir. I participated in Colorado's All-State Jazz Choir, and I was one of only 8 men accepted into it in 2015.

Music Production

I dabble a little in music production from time to time. I enjoy crafting predictable, full-sounding music that relies heavily on repeated melodic patterns. I get plenty of mileage out of chip noises and cheap MIDI synths, which I use to create kitsch, synthesized music meant to evoke a neo-Nintendo nostalgia for the listener. I've posted some of my work on my SoundCloud if you're interested in experiencing it for yourself.

Theatre

I've been involved in community theatre as long as I can remember. Some of my recent roles include Mark in Rent and Anthony in Sweeney Todd. For more details, see my theatre résumé.
