My name's Jacob and I've been involved in musical performances all my life. I'm a singer, pianist, and amatuer music producer. Some of my work and accomplishments are detailed below.

A Cappella

Since my freshman year of college, I've been involved in an a cappella group called In the Buff. This year I've taken over as Music Director, which has been a whirlwind but I'm enjoying it and we sound as great as ever!

ITB has a lot of irons in the fire right now. We turn 25 this year, and our anniversary concert is going to be one of the biggest events we've put on in years! We're also putting the finishing touches on our 6th album, which we're releasing this year.

Getting involved in a cappella was hands-down the best decision I've made in college. I met my closest friends through a cappella. It's something that I get to look forward to throughout my week and it's where I feel most at home.

Music Production

I dabble a little in music production from time to time. I enjoy crafting predictable, full-sounding music that relies heavily on repeated melodic patterns. I get plenty of mileage out of chip noises and cheap MIDI synths, which I use to create kitsch, synthesized music meant to evoke a neo-Nintendo nostalgia for the listener. I've posted some of my work on my SoundCloud if you're interested in experiencing it for yourself.

Theatre

I've been involved in community theatre as long as I can remember. Some of my recent roles include Mark in Rent and Anthony in Sweeney Todd. For more details, see my theatre résumé.

Other
