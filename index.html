About Me

Hey, I'm Jacob! I'm a junior studying Computer Science at University of Colorado Boulder. I'm already putting my schooling to use as a ServiceNow developer for the campus. I've been working in JavaScript since I was little, and I'm very comfortable with it. I specialize in web design and I know a thing or two about HTML/CSS, but if you're into servers I've played with Node.js as well. I have experience in Python and C++ as well, and if you pay me to I'll probably program in them.

I'm also a musician. My a cappella group, In the Buff, competed in 2017's ICCA (the competition from Pitch Perfect) and came in third in the regional quarterfinal with an award for best arrangement. We're currently in the process of recording an album (which I'm super excited about). I dabble in music production on my own, which you can hear for yourself on my SoundCloud. I've played piano since I was little and I'm often involved in community theatre. I'm into jazz as well! Once a month, I sing the oldies at a jam session called Boulder Friends of Jazz.
