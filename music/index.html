Music

My name is Jacob and music has been a major part of my life for as long as I can remember. I'm a singer, pianist, and amatuer music producer. Some of my work and accomplishments are detailed below.

Singing

CU Boulder - In The Buff Men's A Cappella (2015 - 2019)

While I was in college, I was involved in an a cappella group called In the Buff. I was involved in admin stuff starting my sophomore yeat, and my senior year I took over as Music Director, which was a great learning experience and I'm proud of what the group accomplished in that time.

2019 was a big year for ITB. It was their 25th anniversary, and dozens of alumni came from across the country to celebrate at their 25th Birthday Concert. They also recorded and released an album called Wax On Wax Off, now available on Spotify or wherever you get your music.

Other

I've played piano since I was little. I'm into jazz as well! Throughout high school, I sang the stadards at a monthy jam session called Boulder Friends of Jazz.

I've amassed a bit of a following on my YouTube channel, where I occasionally post karaoke tracks, ukulele tutorials, or school projects.
